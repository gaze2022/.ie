ABOUT
Our Festival Friends hold a special place in GAZE's heart, many returning year after year to renew their investment in the GAZE mission — to celebrate the power of LGBT stories on film. We would like to thank all of our Festival Friends and call out to new friends to join up and help make GAZE 2021, and GAZE 2022, (our upcoming 30th) the best festivals yet. Our Festival Friends are a major contributor to the ongoing success of the festival, so as a thank you, we've put together a special package just for you. It's a steal and we shouldn't be giving it away at this price but we love you so stop it, c'mere, go on — we'll be your best friend!
